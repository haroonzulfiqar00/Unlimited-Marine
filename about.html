Our Story

Rooted in Boating, Built on Experience

Unlimited Marine has become a trusted name on Lake Cumberland by delivering exceptional marine sales and services with a personal touch. Owners John and Kim Stivers bring decades of combined experience in marine service and sales—over 75 years of service expertise and 40 years in sales—giving customers confidence that they’re working with knowledgeable professionals who understand boats inside and out.

Founded to serve boaters on the west end of beautiful Lake Cumberland, Unlimited Marine stands out as the only full-service facility in its area, offering everything from new and pre-owned boats to repair, parts, accessories, and specialized services.

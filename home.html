WELCOME TO UNLIMITED MARINE

John and Kim Stivers, owners of Unlimited Marine, welcome you to the only full-service marine sales and service facility on the west end of beautiful Lake Cumberland. With over 75 years of combined experience in quality marine service and more than 40 years in marine sales, our team is committed to delivering expert workmanship and customer service second to none. We are factory-trained and authorized sales and service dealers for MerCruiser, Mercury Outboard, Honda Outboard, Yamaha Outboard, Volvo Penta gasoline engines and drives, and Westerbeke generators. Our technicians utilize cutting-edge diagnostic technology, including the PicoScope system, to accurately diagnose and repair any issue. As area sales representatives for Harris Pontoons and Xpress Aluminum Boats, we also carry a full line of marine parts and accessories to meet all of your boating needs at competitive prices.
